Canteen Management System - FAQ

How do I create an account in the canteen management system? +
To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. Fill in your details, including your name, email, and password. Once registered, you'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.
How do I log in to the system? +
To log in, click on the "Login" button on the homepage. Enter your registered email and password. If you've forgotten your password, click on "Forgot Password" to reset it.
How do I place an order? +
After logging in, browse through the menu, select the items you want, and add them to your cart. Once you're ready, go to your cart, review your order, and click "Place Order." You can choose to pay online or in cash.
Can I modify my order after placing it? +
Orders cannot be modified once placed. However, you can cancel the order before it is accepted by the admin and place a new one.
How do I make a payment? +
Payments can be made online via secure payment gateways integrated into the system. If you prefer to pay in cash, you can select the "Cash Payment" option during checkout, and the admin will accept your request after receiving the payment.
How do I view the details of my order? +
You can view your order details by going to the "My Orders" section. This will show you the status, items ordered, and payment details.
How do I track my order status? +
After placing an order, you can track its status in the "My Orders" section. The status will update in real-time from "Order Placed" to "Processing," "Ready," and finally "Completed."
What if my order is delayed or not delivered? +
If your order is delayed, please contact the canteen admin directly through the "Contact Us" section. You can also check your order status for any updates.
How do I update my account details? +
To update your account details, log in and go to the "Profile" section. Here, you can change your email, password, and other personal information.
Can I cancel my order? +
Yes, you can cancel your order before the admin has accepted it. Once the order is accepted and processing begins, cancellation is no longer possible.
What should I do if I encounter a technical issue? +
If you encounter any technical issues, please report them through the "Support" section. Provide a brief description of the problem, and our team will assist you as soon as possible.
How do I provide feedback or suggestions? +
We value your feedback! You can provide feedback or suggestions through the "Feedback" section in your profile. Your input helps us improve our services.
